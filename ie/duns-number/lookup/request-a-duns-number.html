Request a Dun & Bradstreet D-U-N-S ® Number

All Registered businesses in the UK and Ireland are automatically issued with a D-U-N-S Number.

There is no charge to obtain a Dun & Bradstreet D-U-N-S Number for your business. Having a D-U-N-S Number can make it easier for your customers and suppliers to do business with you, some customers even require it of their suppliers.

A Dun & Bradstreet D-U-N-S Number is a unique nine-digit code assigned by D&B to identify your business.

Find out more about the Dun & Bradstreet D-U-N-S Number and how it can help your business.

If you would like to obtain a Dun & Bradstreet D-U-N-S Number for your business in the UK or Republic of Ireland, and have already tried to find it using the Find my DUNS Number lookup, please provide the following information.

We will respond within five working days.

 

For a D-U-N-S Number for a business in any other country, please contact customer services.
