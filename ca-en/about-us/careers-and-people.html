People & Careers
Life@D&BWe’re at a transformational moment in our company’s history.With almost 2 centuries of experience and a new modern vibe, our work has never been more exhilarating. We’re wildly passionate about our purpose, and it has us transforming everything we do – from how we engage with our customers to how we energize one another. Life@D&B is changing, for the better.Read on for the full story
Our TeamsWe’re getting more fluid, agile and fun!Our global community of about 5,000 people can be found working from corporate offices or plugging in from home desks; listening to our customers and collaborating on solutions; working in new ways and testing the limits of our own thinking. We’re continuously improving and constantly experimenting.Learn more about us
Your GrowthWe’re building a culture of curiosity and learning. You in?We know our people are our greatest asset, and we are putting our money where our mouth is. We’re so excited about our new Learning & Development programs where people of all levels can select from a variety of classes that will help them grow and develop in the directions they are most passionate about.Learn More
Your BenefitsOur rewards and benefits just keep getting better.From the basics, including health and life insurance, and retirement and savings, to family planning and supporting your fitness and well-being, we’ve got you covered. And because we are all about ‘sustainable high performance’, we’re always looking for new ways to help our employees strike that right ‘balance’ in their lives.
