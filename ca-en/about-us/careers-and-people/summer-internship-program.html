Our 2018 Summer Internship Program

If you think that an internship with our company will consist of making copies and going on coffee runs, think again. Our interns engage in challenging and meaningful work, applying what they've learned at school in a real business setting. Our four month internship program runs in many areas including Sales, Marketing, Technology, Sales Operations, Project Management, Customer Relations, Human Resources, Strategy, and Analytics.

See below for more of the details. If you're intrigued, email your resume for consideration to our People Team: UniversityTalent@dnb.com.

The Opportunity
  • Gain relevant work experience, while working alongside leaders and team members on challenging projects.
  • Demonstrate your talent and abilities by applying knowledge acquired through college courses.
  • Network with Senior Leaders
  • Enjoy weekly team building and festive social events throughout the summer.
  • Position yourself for a potential full-time offer.

Your Qualifications
  • Candidates must be currently enrolled in a university or college program
  • Overall minimum B average.
  • Strong organizational, decision making, and analytical skills.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
Entry-Level Opportunities
